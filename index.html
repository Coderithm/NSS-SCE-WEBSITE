NSS KIIT HOME PAGE

Menu

The National Service Scheme (NSS) is a public service program launched by the Government of India in 1969, under the Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports. It aims to develop the personality and character of students through voluntary community service. NSS volunteers work on various activities like cleanliness drives, health camps, awareness campaigns, and rural development, promoting the motto "Not Me, But You". The scheme encourages youth to become socially responsible citizens and contribute to nation-building.

Thank You for Reading

School Visit

NSS volunteers visited a local school to engage with students through interactive sessions and fun learning activities. We conducted awareness programs on hygiene, discipline, and moral values, aiming to inspire young minds and promote holistic development.

📅 December 2025 📍 Damana High School

Slum Visit

NSS volunteers conducted a slum visit to interact with the residents and understand their challenges. We organized awareness sessions on hygiene, education, and health, and also distributed essential items. The visit aimed to uplift the community through care, knowledge, and social responsibility.

📅 March 2025 📍 Local Slum

DAAN

NSS volunteers organized a Daan (donation) drive to support underprivileged communities. Essentials like clothes, food items, and stationery were collected and distributed with compassion. The initiative aimed to promote the spirit of sharing and make a positive difference in the lives of those in need.

📅 November 2024 📍 Slum

Special Camp

NSS volunteers successfully conducted a Special Camp focused on community development and social awareness. Throughout the camp, various activities such as cleanliness drives, awareness rallies, health check-ups, and educational sessions were organized. The camp aimed to instill a sense of responsibility, service, and leadership among volunteers while addressing local community needs.

📅 March 2024 📍 Village

Animal Feeding

NSS volunteers conducted an animal feeding drive to show compassion towards stray animals. We provided food and clean water to dogs, cows, and other animals in the locality, promoting kindness and care for voiceless beings. The activity aimed to foster empathy and encourage humane treatment of animals in the community.

📅 November 2024 📍 KIIT Road

Road Safety Rally

NSS volunteers organized a road safety rally to spread awareness about traffic rules and responsible driving. Through posters, slogans, and public interaction, we educated people on the importance of wearing helmets, seatbelts, and following traffic signals. The rally aimed to promote a culture of safety and reduce road accidents.

📅 January 2025 📍 KIIT Road

Cleanliness Drive

NSS volunteers actively participated in a cleanliness drive to promote hygiene and environmental awareness. We cleaned public areas, spread awareness about waste management, and encouraged the community to maintain a clean and healthy environment. The initiative reflected our commitment to the Swachh Bharat Abhiyan and community well-being.

📅 September 2024 📍 KIIT Road

Plantation Drive

NSS volunteers conducted a plantation drive to promote environmental sustainability and awareness. Saplings were planted in public spaces and educational institutions, encouraging green practices and responsibility towards nature. The drive aimed to contribute to a cleaner, greener, and healthier environment.

📅 July 2025 📍 Prasanti Vihar

Orphange Visit

NSS volunteers visited a local orphanage to engage with the children and share meaningful moments. We conducted interactive sessions where we taught them good habits, hygiene practices, and moral values through fun activities. The visit aimed to inspire, educate, and bring smiles to their faces while reinforcing our commitment to community service.

📅 March 2025 📍 Madhurmaye Orphange

Health Camp

NSS SCE organized an ENT health camp to spread awareness and provide basic healthcare support to the community. The camp offered free check-ups, consultations, and guidance on ENT-related issues. Through this initiative, we aimed to promote early detection, encourage healthy practices, and serve society with compassion and responsibility.

📅 March 2025 📍 Damana High School
